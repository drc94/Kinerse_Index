About the Game

Andromeda Sports is a virtual reality (VR) fitness game designed to merge the excitement of gaming with the benefits of physical exercise. Players use elastic resistance bands for a unique workout experience, while riding hoverboards through stunning, futuristic environments.

Features

  • Immersive VR gameplay with stunning visuals
  • Elastic resistance bands for enhanced physical activity
  • Procedurally generated obstacle courses
  • Customizable training modes
  • Available as a demo on the Meta Quest platform
